Know about our team

core values

We think and act as Global Leaders. We set high professional standards and adhere to those standards. Actively seek engagement in transport & logistics sector. Drive growth with energy and passionate attitude. Act like a global family.

Who We Are?

The Chartered Institute of Logistics and Transport Bangladesh is a robust platform of qualified professionals with the mindset to help. With our support, professionals from various industries grow and excel in their career. Our respected heritage and comprehensive knowledge makes us leaders in planning and building the road to success for those seeking career development within the field. We believe in being clear and responsive to the community’s needs in order to create leaders who can carry on the legacy.

What we do

Create models of growth and network our members can benefit from

Arrange seminars, workshops, lectures etc. on recent developments contemporary issues in our sector

Share best practices within the industry

Bring a global and international experience to a local community

Maintain a comprehensive online library for knowledge sharing (Global and Local)

Create a talented pool of professionals through extensive training

Passionately serve our members

Our History

1919

The Institute of Transport was founded to meet the very real need for professional transport systems demonstrated during WW1.

1926

Granted Royal Charter and called the Chartered Insitute of Transport (CIT)

1982

First Logistics and Transport Focus magazine

1997

Her Royal Highness The Princess Royal has agreed to be Patron of the new Institute of Logistics and Transport, thus countinuing her association with the two former Institutes, IoL and CIT UK.

1999

The first stand of The Institute of Logistics and Transport (ILT) was a prime attraction at the Metropole Hotel, London which ran alongside The Logistics Conference.

1999

CIT Bangladesh Council has stared journey by the Founder President Rear Admiral M.H. Khan psc, BN, FCILT, .

2001

We became the Chartered Institute of Logistics and Transport (CILT) as the two leading professional bodies joined together.

2014

Offering the globally recognized CILT International Diploma in Logistics and Transport (Level 5) in Bangladesh with its accredited training provider.

2017

Mr. Muhammad Refayet Chowdhury, MILT a Young Professional Member of CILT Bangladesh Council was a finalist and achieved 2nd position on International Young Achiever 2017 during the CILT International Convention in Macao

2019

Mr. Mohiuddin Abdul Kadir, FCILT FNI FCIArb, has been elected as President of CILT Bangladesh Council for the term 2019-2021.
