The people are the key driving force that makes CILT BD what it is today. Whether you are a student building a career or a professional trying to expand your business or skills, we have something for everybody.

Membership Benefits

  • A post-nominal designation of your grade providing international recognition as a professional in logistics and transport

  • Access to our website, databases, knowledge centers and best global practices.

  • Vast international networking and CPD opportunities with other professionals in the institute (Approximately over 35,000 members)

  • The chance to further your career with both our education and qualification programmes.

Code Of Conduct

  • INTEGRITY : Act with integrity and professionalism and carry out their duties in such a way as to promote a positive image of the Institute and their profession

  • RESPONSIBILITY : Act responsibly to secure the welfare, health and safety of all members of their organisations and take account of the impact of their activities on the environment and the community

  • INSPIRE : Continue their professional development throughout their careers and actively assist and encourage fellow members, as well as non-members, to advance their knowledge and expertise

  • PERSIST : Endeavour to serve the interests of their employers and employees without compromising professional integrity or ethics

  • STRIVE : Endeavour to serve the interests of their employers and employees without compromising professional integrity or ethics

  • Promote : Actively international understanding, goodwill and co-operation
