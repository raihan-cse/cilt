YOUNG PROFESSIONALS

Be a part of our team

The International Young Professionals exists to strengthen the appeal of CILT to young professionals working in the logistics and transport sectors ds

We must not just enable today’s leaders but nurture tomorrow’s

HOW DO YOU JOIN?

CILT members under 35 years of age are automatically members of the Young Professionals Group.

Join Young Professionals

Membership Benefits

  • A post-nominal designation of your grade providing international recognition as a professional in logistics and transport

  • Access to our website, databases, knowledge centers and best global practices.

  • Vast international networking and CPD opportunities with other professionals in the institute (Approximately over 35,000 members

  • The chance to further your career with both our education and qualification programmes.
