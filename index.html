To Build A Better & A More Solid Bangladesh

For 20 years, CILT Bangladesh has been building a community: one that will build a better and a more solid Bangladesh. With almost 100 years of knowledge from a global institute, we are the first choice and trusted partner for anyone planning to build a career in transport and logistics.

BANGLADESH JOINED THE GLOBAL WiLAT FAMILY AS ITS 22ND CHAPTER

Chartered Institute of Logistics and Transport (CILT) Bangladesh launched its Women’s Forum, Women in Logistics and Transport (WiLAT), on August 21, 2020.
